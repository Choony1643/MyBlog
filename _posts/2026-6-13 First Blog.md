折腾了一阵，我的个人博客终于开通了。
After some tinkering, my personal blog is finally up and running.

为什么突然想写博客？记录一下自己学过的看过的东西，不至于草草地度过太多的时间

This blog is built with Jekyll + GitHub Pages, and the whole process was as straightforward as filling in the blanks.

好了，这是个新的开始，希望自己能坚持写下去。
Alright, this is a new beginning. Hope I can stick with it.
